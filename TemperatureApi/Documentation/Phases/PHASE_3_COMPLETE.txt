   └─ Enforces HTTPS (HSTS) for 1 year

6. Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin

   └─ Disables browser feature access

   Command: ./backup-database.sh restore backups/iot_db_20260308_120000.sql
   Process: Full database restoration with confirmation
   Time: 2-5 minutes (depends on data size)
   Validation: Automatic integrity check post-restore
